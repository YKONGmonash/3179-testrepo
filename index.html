Australian Wildfire Brightness Temperature Data

From the visualisation below, you can see the distribution of data points collected by NASA satellites which is publically available.
The data used here is from the VIIRS (Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite), an instrument that provides near real-time (NRT) imagery.
The higher the brightness temperature, the larger the circle and redder it appears on the map of Australia below.
From the map, we can see that the points are clustered around the edges of the Australian coasts, with only a few points around the centre-west area.
